## Black Bean Soup

- Heat Â¼ c. oil on high in the soup pot
- Chop (bite size) and add, in this order:

1 Red Onion

1 Bunch Celery

2 Large Carrots

2 Large peppers or 6 small peppers

18 Cloves Garlic

- Strain, rinse thoroughly, and add 3 cans of black beans *#3.5F*
- Add 2 cans of tomatoes with chiles, with juice
- Add 1 can of sweet corn, with juice
- Add veg broth until the pot is full
- Season: 2 TB lime juice
  - 2 TB cilantro
  - 1 TB taco seasoning
  - 1 tsp garlic salt seasoning
  - 1 tsp black pepper
  - 1 tsp salt
