# GF Monster Cookie

## Ingredients

- 3 3# peanut butter
- 6 sticks butter, softened
- 3.3# brown sugar
- 2.64# white sugar
- 12 eggs
- 1 tbsp vanilla
- 3 TBSP baking soda
- 3 tsp salt
- 5.67# GF Oats
- 3 c. M&Ms
- 3 c. Chocolate chips

## Instructions

1. Beat butter, PB, and sugars on #3 til smooth.
2. Whip in eggs and vanilla on #3
3. On #1 stir in oats, baking soda and salt
4. On #1 stir in m&ms and choccy chips
5. Puck immediately with 3.5 Oz scoop
