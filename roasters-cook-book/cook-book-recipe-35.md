## Pickled Veg

## Ingredients

- ½ yellow onion, thinly sliced
- 1 bell pepper thinly sliced
- ~~8oz red wine vinegar~~
- 10oz white wine vinegar
- 6oz water
- 1 tea bag "pickled veg seasoning"
- 3 star anise
- 4 cloves garlic

**Handwritten notes:**

- "not enough - just sav[ory]"
- "I got coriander seed or all together"
- "toss all together"

## Instructions

Add onion, peppers and star anise to a jar. Boil vinegars. Add tea bag of seasoning to jar and pour vinegar mix into jar. Let jar sit uncovered til cooled. Refrigerate!
