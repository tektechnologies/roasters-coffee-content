## Chocolate Chip Cookie

## Ingredients

- 12 sticks butter, softened
- 2.64# white sugar
- 2.64# brown sugar
- 12 eggs
- 4 TBSP vanilla extract
- 5.94# flour
- 2 TBSP baking soda
- 1 TBSP salt
- 1-4.5 Lb. Bag chocolate chips

## Instructions

1. Cream sugar and butter on "2" for about 5 minutes
2. Whip in eggs and vanilla on "2"
3. Turn speed back to "1" and gradually mix in all dry ingredients
4. Use 3.25 oz scoop and puck cookies

*For good measure go back in and mix anything stuck in the bottom of mixer*
