## How Do I Bake....

## Baking Temperatures and Times

**Chocolate Chip:** 350 degrees for 10-12 minutes

**Dark Chocolate Walnut:** 350 degrees for 10-12 minutes

**Peanut Butter:** 350 degrees for 10-12 minutes

**Molasses:** 350 degrees for 10-12 minutes

**Gluten Free Monster:** 350 degrees for 10-12 minutes

**\*Honey Bourbon Butterscotch:\*** 375 degrees for 10-12 minutes

**Scones:** 325 degrees for 20-22 minutes.

**Gluten Free Oat Bars:** 300 degrees for 12-15 minutes

**Beef:** 350 degrees for 30 minutes. Check if the internal temperature has reached 145 degrees. If it hasn't reached 145, continue baking until it does.

**Chicken:** 350 degrees for 20 minutes or until it reaches an internal temperature of 165 degrees.

**Bacon:** 350 degrees for 10-12 minutes (we only want to par-cook bacon, we reheat it for service when its ordered)

**Quiche Crust (Puff Pastry):** 350 degrees, for 10-15 minutes or until golden brown.
