## Oatmeal Raisin Cookies

## Ingredients

- [ ] 12 sticks butter, softened
- [ ] 2.64 # white sugar
- [ ] 2.64 # brown sugar
- [ ] 12 eggs
- [ ] 4 tbsp vanilla
- [ ] 3.3# flour
- [ ] 2 TBSP baking soda
- [ ] 1 TBSP salt
- [ ] 1 TBSP cinnamon
- [ ] 3.3# oats
- [ ] 6 c. raisins

## Ingredients (Second Column)

- [ ] 12 sticks butter
- [ ] 1# brown sugar
- [ ] 1# white sugar
- [ ] 6 eggs
- [ ] 2 tbsp vanilla
- [ ] 1.65# flour
- [ ] 1 tsp baking soda
- [ ] 1/2 tsp salt
- [ ] 1/2 tbsp cinnamon
- [ ] 1.65# oats
- [ ] 3 c. raisins

## Directions

1. Cream butter and sugars on "2"
2. Add in eggs and vanilla
3. Turn speed back down to "1" and gradually add all dry ingredients
4. Use a 3.25 oz scoop and puck cookies
