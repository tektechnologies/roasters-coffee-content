## Garlic Herb'd Butter

- 1 stick butter, softened
- 1 tsp salt
- ½ tsp white pepper
- 1 tsp thyme
- 1tsp parsley
- ¼ tsp rosemary powder
- 4 cloves garlic, minced and roasted
