**Raspberry:** 1 c. sugar, 1 c. water, 2 tsp raspberry extract, ¼ tsp citric acid solution

**Vanilla:** 8 c. sugar, 8 c. water, 2/3 c. vanilla extract

**CHAI:** 4 c. brown sugar (1.76#), 8 c. water, 2 c. honey (1.4#), 1 c. vanilla anise BT, 1 tsp clove, 1 TB cardamom, 2 TB anise seed, 1 TB pink pepper, 1 TB green pepper, ¼ c. cinnamon, ¼ c. coriander, ½ c. ginger, ½ c. black pepper. Heat to boiling, let steep for 12 hours before straining.

## Spring/Summer Seasonal

**Coconut:** 1 c. sugar, 1 c. water, 2 tsp coconut extract, ¼ tsp citric acid solution

**Lemongrass:** 1 c. sugar, 1 c. water, 2 TB lemongrass, ¼ tsp citric acid solution

**Orange:** 1 c. sugar, 1 c. water, 2 tsp orange flavoring, 1 tsp coriander, ¼ tsp citric acid solution

**Rhubarb:** 1 c. sugar, 1 c. water, 2/3 c chopped rhubarb, ¼ tsp citric acid solution

**Blackberry Sage:** 1 c. sugar, 1 c. water, 1 TB blackberry extract, ½ TB sage, ¼ tsp citric acid solution

**Rosemary:** 1 c. sugar, 1 c. water, 2 TB rosemary, ¼ tsp citric acid solution
