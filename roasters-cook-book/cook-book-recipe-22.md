## Cream Cheese/Fruit Danishes

## Cream cheese filling

- 32 oz cream cheese, softened
- 2/3 c. sour cream, room temp
- Zest of one lemon
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 tsp lemon juice
- 1/8 tsp salt
- 1 c. sugar
- 4 large eggs

Use stand mixer to beat until smooth, adding eggs one at a time incorporating each individually

## Process for Crust

Thaw sheets of puff pastry, cut into 8 squares with pizza cutter, fit into muffin tins and bake for 12 minutes 325°. Cool, fill with cream cheese filling, bake 12 minutes 325°. Add fruit topping, bake 5 mi
