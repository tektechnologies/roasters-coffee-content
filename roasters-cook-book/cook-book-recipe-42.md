## Syrups

Syrups do not need to boil, however sugar must be melted completely and spices thoroughly stirred in. Strain out any solids before bottling. 30 day shelf life.

**Citric Acid Solution: 1 c. water, 1 TB citric acid**

**Anise:** 1 c. sugar, 1 c. water, 1 tsp vanilla anise BT, 1 tsp anise seed, ¼ tsp citric acid solution

**Black Forest Berry:** 1 c. sugar, 1 c. water, 2 TB black forest herbal tea, ¼ tsp citric acid solution

**Cinnamon:** 1 c. sugar, 1 c. water, 1 tsp ground Ceylon cinnamon, ¼ tsp citric acid solution

**Hazelnut:** 1 c. sugar, 1 c. water, 2 tsp hazelnut extract, ¼ tsp citric acid solution

**Honey Ginger:** ¾ c. sugar, 1 c. water, ¼ c. honey, 1.5 TB ginger, ¼ tsp citric acid solution

**Lavender:** 1 c. sugar, 1 c. water, 1 TB lavender, ¼ tsp citric acid solution

**Peppermint:** 1c. sugar, 1 c. water, 1 TB peppermint, ¼ tsp citric acid solution
