## Peanut Butter Cookies

## Ingredients

- [ ] 12 sticks of butter, softened
- [ ] 1.65 # peanut butter
- [ ] 2.64# white sugar
- [ ] 2.64# brown sugar
- [ ] 12 eggs
- [ ] 4.95# flour
- [ ] 2 Tbsp baking powder
- [ ] 3 Tbsp baking soda
- [ ] 1 Tbsp salt
- [ ] 3 C. Peanuts

## Instructions

1. Cream sugars, butter and peanut butter on "2"
2. Beat eggs in on "2"
3. Turn speed back to "1" and gradually add all dry ingredients. Make sure everything is incorporated properly
4. Use 3.25 oz scoop and puck cookies
