## Daily, Weekly & Monthly Cleaning Tasks

*Doesn't matter what days these things get done, just make sure they are getting done please. Date & Initial!*

*If you do extra tasks beyond this list please write down & intial*

## Daily: (See closing list for more tasks)

- Wipe out line @ close
- Wipe soup wells
- Wipe toaster/salamander/oven
- If time allows, send your own prep dishes through
- Stock paper goods
- Sanitize knives
- Counters, duh :P
- "Cafiza" coffee carafes
- Sweep/mop

## Weekly

- Wipe down fridges, inside & out. Condense/toss expired product
- Deep clean line (Wipe down dressing bottles, wash seasoning containers & tubs)
- Wipe down syrup bottles, send syrup tub through dishwasher, refill as needed.
- Wipe out grab n go/ stock as needed
- Pull out coolers, sweep and mop

## Monthly

- Wipe down/ organize shelves in FOH and BOH. Toss anything past expiration
- Clean out freezer, toss anything past expiration or freezer burnt.
