## Banana Bars (Triple Batch)

## Ingredients

- 4 flax eggs (9 TB flax, 11.25 oz hot water)
- 13.5 c. GF oats (3.17#)
- 2 ¼ c. brown sugar (1.25#)
- 3 TB baking powder
- 1 TB vanilla extract
- 4 ½ c. soy milk
- 9 ripe bananas
- 2 ¼ c. PB (1.23#)
- 1 ½ c. peanuts (.5#)
- ~~1½ c. walnuts (.4#)~~

## Instructions

1. Make flax eggs. Preheat oven 300°.
2. Combine all ingredients, mix well.
3. Transfer immediately into 3 wax paper lined half baking sheets.
4. Bake for 12 min, rotate, additional 12 min.
5. Let cool
