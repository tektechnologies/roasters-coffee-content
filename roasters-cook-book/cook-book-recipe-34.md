## Chicken for Line

Season with:

- Salt
- Pepper
- Onion
- Garlic

Bake at 350 degrees til chicken reaches an internal temperature of 165

---

## Roast Beef

Rub down beef with "Beef Rub" til coated. Bake at 400 til beef reaches an internal temperature of 145. Let rest 24 hours and thinly slice.
