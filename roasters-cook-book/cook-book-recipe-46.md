## Chai

## Ingredients

Fill 2 tea bags with:

- 2 tsp Cinnamon
- 2 tsp Ginger
- 1 tsp clove
- 1 tsp nutmeg
- 1tsp fennel
- 1tsp all spice
- 1tsp cardamom
- ¼ tsp white pepper
- ¼ tsp cayenne

## Instructions

Place tea bags in about 1 gallon of water, let boil for 20 minutes

Add 1 cup loose leaf chai and steep for 5 minutes.

Strain well, serve sweetened with honey
