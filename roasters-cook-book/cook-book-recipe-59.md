## Pesto Chicken Salad

1. Toast 2 pieces of bread
2. Add pesto and (1) swiss on top slice
3. Add (1) swiss to bottom slice
4. Add 3.5oz scoop of chix salad
5. Top w/ red onion
   - celery
   - romaine
