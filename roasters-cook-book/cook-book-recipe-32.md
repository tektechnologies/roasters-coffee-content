## Sweet Potato Lentil Stew

- Heat ¼ c. oil on high in soup pot
- Chop (bite size) and add, in this order:
  - 1 Red Onion
  - 1 Bunch Celery
  - 2 Large Sweet Potatoes
  - 1 Large Carrot
  - 13 Cloves Garlic
- Add Veg Broth until pot is ¾ full
- Cook on high 1 hour or until sweet potatoes and carrots are softened
- Add lentils ← 8 oz (½ bag)
- Season
  - 6 bay leaves
  - 1 TB garlic salt seasoning
  - 1 tsp salt
  - 1 tsp black pepper
  - 1 tea ball green peppercorns
  - 1 tsp paprika
  - ½ tsp cumin
  - ½ tsp coriander
  - ¼ tsp turmeric
  - ½ tsp Lemon juice
  - 1 tsp thyme
- Reduce heat to hold temp, 2 or 3
