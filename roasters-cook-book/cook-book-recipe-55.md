## Bacon, Eggs, Toast

1. Toast 2 pieces of bread + Add butter
2. Heat 4 slices of bacon in oven
3. Fry eggs to order (over easy, over med, over hard)
4. Serve w/ jelly
5. If subbing Turkey Sausage (2 patties)
