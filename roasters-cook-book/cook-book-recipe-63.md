## Chocolate Chip Peanut Butter Muffins

## Ingredients

- ½ cup Vegetable or Canola Oil
- ⅔ cup Peanut Butter
- 1 cup Brown Sugar
- 2 Eggs
- 1 ¼ cup Milk
- 2 TSP Vanilla
- 2 TSP Baking Powder
- ½ TSP Baking Soda
- ½ TSP Salt
- 2 cups Flour
- 1 ¼ cup Chocolate Chips (or sub ½ cup with mini peanut butter chips)

## Instructions

1. In a bowl, mix oil, peanut butter, brown sugar and eggs
2. Add milk and vanilla to the mixture
3. In a separate bowl, mix baking powder, baking soda, salt, and flour
4. Add ingredients to the wet mixture
5. Fold in chocolate/peanut butter chips
6. When they are complete and cooled, drizzle with chocolate or peanut butter

## Baking

Bake at 375. Once they rise turn temp down to 350.

10 minutes bake, flip, 10 minute bake then check to see if they are cooked through.
