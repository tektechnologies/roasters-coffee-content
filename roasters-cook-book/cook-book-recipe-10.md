## Honey Bourbon Butterscotch

## Ingredients

- [ ] 16 sticks butter, softened
- [ ] 1.76 # white sugar
- [ ] 3.88 # brown sugar
- [ ] 16 eggs
- [ ] 3 C. Honey Whiskey
- [ ] 1 Tbsp + 1 tsp vanilla
- [ ] 3.5 # regular oats
- [ ] 4.4 # flour
- [ ] 5 Tbsp + 1 tsp baking soda
- [ ] 2 Tbsp + 2 tsp salt
- [ ] 10 c. butterscotch chips

## Instructions

1. Cream butter and sugars on "2"
2. Slowly add in eggs, vanilla and whiskey
3. Turn speed down to "1" gradually add in dry ingredients
4. Use a 3.25 oz scoop and puck cookies
