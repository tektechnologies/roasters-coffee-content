## Banana Bars

## Ingredients

- 4.5 c. Oats
- ¾ c. Brown Sugar
- 1 Tbsp. Baking Powder
- 1 Tsp. Vanilla
- 1 ½ c. Soy milk
- 3 ripe Bananas
- ⅓ c. Peanut Butter (.41 lbs.)
- ½ c. VEGAN chocolate chips
- ⅓ c. peanuts
- ½ c. walnuts
- 3 Flax eggs

## Directions

1. Make Flax eggs (see flax egg recipe)
2. Preheat oven 300 degrees.
3. Combine all ingredients.
4. Line a half baking sheet with wax paper.
5. Bake for 12 minutes and rotate pan.
6. Bake for another 12 minutes.
7. VOILA!
