## Aioli

- 4 eggs
- 1 lemon (juiced)
- 1 tsp salt
- ¼ tsp cayenne
- 6 cloves garlic (roasted)
- 1 ½ canola oil
- ¼ water

Combine all ingredients in blender, except oil and water. Blend together. While mixing slowly add 1 cup of canola oil, add water to remaining oil, pour into blender.
