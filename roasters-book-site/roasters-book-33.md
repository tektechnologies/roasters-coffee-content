## Closing Procedure

## Steps

### 1. Count Drawer

Count drawer, add total of bills and coins on the line labelled **"Grand Total"**

### 2. Calculate Deposit

Subtract **$300.00** from Grand Total and write the difference on the next line. This is the **deposit**.

   **a.** Take the cash amount of the Deposit and put it in an empty bank bag

   **b.** Write the Deposit figure on a bank deposit slip and place it in the bank bag

   **c.** If there are any checks in the register, place them in the bank bag and record their check numbers and dollar amounts on the bank slip as well

### 3. Record Credit Card Tips

Add the total amount of credit card tips taken from the register and record the sum on the next line labelled **"Credit Card Tips"**

- If we record if tips is missing, the amount can be found under:
  - **Reports → Sales**
  - Press the text labelled **"Show Details"**

### 4. Calculate the Difference

Go to the **"Reports"** menu on the Square Register and scroll down to the section labelled **"Sales by Payment Type"**

On the line labelled **"Cash"** where is a green bar:

- Subtract figure from the previous line (Credit Card Tips)
- Write the difference on the line **"the Difference"**

### 5. End Drawer

Add **$300.00** to the line **"the Difference"** and:

- Go to **Reports → Current Drawer → End Drawer**
- Enter this sum on the line **"Actual in Drawer"**
- Confirm the drawer close
- Put the slip of paper that prints out in the bank bag

### 6. Final Steps

- Record the Deposit and the Difference on the sheets on the side of the fridge
- Lock the register drawer and bank bags in the black cabinet
