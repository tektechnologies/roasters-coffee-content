## Drink Special Recipes

**\* = Holiday Drinks**

- **Cherry Cordial** - 1 pump Cherry, 1 pump Chocolate, 1 pump Amaretto
- **\*White Rose** - 1 ½ pump White Chocolate, 1 ½ Rasberry
- **Mocha Berry** - 1 ½ pump Chocolate, 1 pump Blackberry, ½ Strawberry
- **\*Pot of Gold** - 1 ½ pump Irish Creme, 1 ½ pump Brown Sugar Cinnamon
- **\*Nutty Pumpkin** - 1 ½ pump Hazelnut, 1 ½ pump Pumpkin Spice
- **\*Dipped Strawberry** - 1 ½ pump Chocolate, 1 ½ pump Strawberry
- **Raspberry Caramel Drop** - 1 ½ pump Raspberry, 1 ½ pump Caramel
- **\*Sinful Valentine** - 1 pump Chocolate, 1 pump Strawberry, 1 pump Raspberry
- **\*S'mores Latte** - 2 pumps Chocolate, 1 pump Toasted Marshmallow
- **Snickerdoodle** - 1 ½ pump Brown Sugar Cinnamon, 1 ½ pump French Vanilla
- **\*Trick or Treat** - 1 ½ pump Salted Caramel, 1 ½ Pumpkin Spice
- **Almond Joy** - 1 pump Chocolate, 1 pump Coconut, 1 pump Almond
- **Nutty Professor** - 1 ½ pump Hazelnut, 1 ½ pump Coconut
- **Vanilla Berry** - 1 ½ pump Vanilla, 1 ½ pump Raspberry
- **Lavender Vanilla** - 1 pump Lavender 2 pump Vanilla
- **Sticky Toffee Caramel** - 1 ½ pump Caramel, 1 ½ pump English Toffee
- **Strawberry Shortcake** - 1 pump Strawberry, 1 pump Vanilla, 1 pump Almond
- **Hazelnut Truffle** - 1 ½ Chocolate, 1 ½ Hazelnut
- **\*Irish White Mocha** - 2 pump White Chocolate, 1 pump Irish Cream
- **\*Ginger Nut Latte** - 1 ½ pump Gingerbread, 1 ½ pump Hazelnut
- **\*Cinnamon Mocha** - 1 ½ pump Cinnamon, 1 ½ pump Chocolate
- **Sugar Cookie Latte** - 1 pump Almond, 1 pump Toasted Marshmallow, 1 pump Vanilla
- **\*Cinnamon Roll Latte** - 1 ½ pump Cinnamon, 1 ½ pump Vanilla
- **\*Pumpkin Brulee** - 1 ½ pump Pumpkin Spice, 1 ½ pump Vanilla
- **Chocolate Covered Raspberry** - 1 ½ pump Chocolate, 1 ½ pump Raspberry/Strawberry *(Same as "Dipped Raspberry")*
- **Blackberry Crumble** - 1 ½ pump Blackberry, 1 ½ pump Almond
- **\*Peppermint Mocha** - 1 pump Peppermint, 2 pump Chocolate
- **Cinnamon Dolce** - 1 ½ pump Cinnamon, 1 ½ pump Caramel
- **Mocha Praline** - 1 pump Chocolate, 1 pump Caramel, 1 pump Hazelnut
