## Drink Recipes

| Drink | 12oz | 16oz | 20oz |
|-------|------|------|------|

| **Mocha** | 1 pump sauce, steamed milk | 2 pumps sauce, steamed milk | 3 pumps sauce, steamed milk |
| **Maxim Mocha** (use aztec cocoa) | 1 shot espresso, 1 scoop powder, steamed milk | 2 shots espresso, 1 1/2 scoops powder, steamed milk | 3 shots espresso, 1 1/2 scoops powder, steamed milk |
| **Ice to** (see above) | 1 shot espresso, 2 pump syrup, steamed milk | 2 shots espresso, 3 pumps syrup, steamed milk | 3 shots espresso, 4 pumps syrup, steamed milk |
| **Depth Charge** | 1 shot espresso, brewed coffee | 2 shots espresso, brewed coffee | 3 shots espresso, brewed coffee |
| **Red Eye** | 2 shots espresso, hot water | 3 shots espresso, hot water | 4 shots espresso, hot water |
| **Americano** | for water | for water | for water |
| **Traditional Macchiato** | 2 shots espresso, dollop of foam | 3 shots espresso, dollop of foam | 4 shots espresso, dollop of foam |
| **Cafe Au Lait** | ¾ coffee with ½ steamed milk | | |
